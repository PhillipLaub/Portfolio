About Me

My name is Phil Laub. I grew up right here in Southern California, and spent most of my life in between Hemet and San Diego. I have a Wife and a five year old daughter, who I spend every waking moment with that I am able. I grew up as a musician, which led me to have such a love for different kinds of music. From Classic Rock to Hip Hop, just please don't subject me to listening to any country music. Some of my favorites include: The Gorillaz, Wu-Tang, The Offspring, Eminem, Green Day, Led Zeppelin, and many more.
I am also an enormous nerd, I love the Marvel franchise, however, I am much more of a DC fan (hopefully they can stop making such a mess of their movies one of these days). I am a big gamer, grew up with era of Super Mario Bros. and Sonic the Hedgehog, nowadays I mostly play first person shooters with my friends whenever I get the chance. I spent more than a decade managing electronic stores, and I am extremely enthusiastic to be stepping forward into a career that is challenging and also provides the opportunity to create. I want to use what I learn to solve problems, make life easier and more convenient, and create something that can bring some joy into someones life.
