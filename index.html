About Me

Recently received certification in Full Stack Development from the University of California Riverside Extension, with skills including MySQL, MongoDB, Node.js, and proficiency with Javascript, CSS, and HTML to create a highly responsive design. Enthusiastic about solving problems and issues that impact the user experience, with an emphasis on creating designs that are built and optimized for mobile. Working with teams on projects has led to the expanding of skills in different areas, exploring different thought processes, and collaboratively reaching a solution that will be the most beneficial for the user. Constantly looking to make a footprint in this technologically driven world, I couldn't be more fortunate to work in an industry that I am passionate about.
